overview: >
  CULTURE Magazine needed a campaign that could communicate their position at the intersection of streetwear, editorial photography and subcultural documentation.
context: >
  The magazine sits between high fashion and street culture. Previous campaigns leaned too heavily in one direction. We needed to find the visual center.
approach: >
  We cast from the street, not from agencies. Locations were real, not styled. The creative direction was about capturing energy, not manufacturing it.
system: >
  A visual language built on contrast — high production meets raw location, precise typography meets candid photography, luxury fabric meets concrete.
execution: >
  Full campaign shoot across 3 Istanbul locations, 8 models, 2 days. Post-production was deliberately minimal. Layout and typography followed the same raw logic.
outcome: >
  The campaign was featured by 5 international fashion platforms. Magazine subscriptions increased 25% in the quarter following launch.
